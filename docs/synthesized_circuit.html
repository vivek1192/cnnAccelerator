Key observation: The delay lines (delay1[0:7], delay2[0:7]) were inferred as 16× SRL16E shift-register LUTs — Vivado's optimal mapping for short fixed-depth FIFOs. Each SRL16E is 1 LUT used as a 16-deep shift register, replacing what would have been 16 FFs per delay line.
SRL16E optimization: Vivado replaced the 16 behavioural shift-register FFs in delay1[] and delay2[] with 16 SRL16E primitives. An SRL16E is a 1-LUT shift register up to 16 deep — more efficient than chained FFs for depths 3–16.
